## 2 Ways to Download Youtube Audio. Easily

If you’d like to**download Youtube audio** , you’ve come to the right place. You can learn**two simple ways** to download Youtube audio below:[Online](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/) &[Offline](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/) . You don’t have to try both ways; just pick the easier way for you.

## Way 1: Download Youtube audio online

 There are many websites allowing you to download Youtube audio directly and freely. If you don’t want to download and install any third-party software, downloading Youtube audio online is a better option for you. To download Youtube audio online, the first thing you need to do is find a website with good reputation. If you’re not sure which website you could trust, you can use[www.onlinevideoconverter.com](https://www.onlinevideoconverter.com/mp3-converter) .

To download Youtube audio, follow steps below:

 1) Go to[www.onlinevideoconverter.com](https://www.onlinevideoconverter.com/mp3-converter)

 2) Enter your Youtube video link, then click the**START** button.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b091325aebea.jpg)

 3) Click the **DOWNLOAD** button. Then the download of audio file (.mp3) will get started.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b0913575085d.jpg)

4) Check to see if you can open the downloaded audio file successfully.

## Way 2: Download Youtube audio with a video converter

 Online converting may cause audio low quality issue. And it’s time-consuming if you need to convert more than one Youtube video to audio. If you want higher quality audio, it’s recommended you use VCL media player.

Just follow these steps:

 1)[Download](https://mirrors.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/videolan-ftp/vlc/2.2.4/win32/vlc-2.2.4-win32.exe) and install VCL media player.

 2) Run the program and click**Media** in top menu bar and select**Convert/Save…**

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b09174238dc0.jpg)

 3) Click**Add…** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b09172866f3b.jpg)

 4) Select the Youtube video file that you’ve saved and click**Open** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b09179431013.jpg)

 5) Click**Convert/Save** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b0917a1dcb7e.jpg)

 6) Choose the audio**Profile** type that you want. As below screenshot, we choose**Audio – MP3** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b0917ac48499.jpg)

 7) Click**Browse** to save the file to a specific location.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b0917bc154dd.jpg)

 8) Click**Start** button then the audio will be saved.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/img_5b0917e09b47a.jpg)
