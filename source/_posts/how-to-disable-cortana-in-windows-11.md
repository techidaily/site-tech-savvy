## Desktop Icon Disappearance Troubles? Here's How to Fix Them for Windows 11

We have received complaints from users saying that the desktop icons for their Windows 10 would somehow disappeared even when they have made no changes to their system whatsoever.

 This remains a mystery why would this happen, but all hopes are not lost. Here are some very easy-to-follow instructions to get your desktop icons restored.

 1) Right-click on the blank spot of your desktop, then choose **Personalize** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/img_588028229b7cc.png)

 2) On the left side of the pane, choose **Themes** , then on the right side, select **Desktop icon settings** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/img_58802e92df990.jpg)

 3) Choose what icons you want to add or remove from your desktop, and then hit **Apply** and **OK** to save and exit.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/img_58802f339c201.png)

 If you are with Windows 7 or Windows 8 operating system, and you also want to restore the icons back to the desktop, here is how you can do it.

 1) Follow the path: **Start** button **\> Control Panel** . View by **Small icons** , and then choose **Personalization** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/img_5880348cc53ea.jpg)

 2) On the left side of the pane, choose **Change desktop icons** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/img_588034ebed73e.png)

 3) Choose the icons you want to add to or remove from the desktop, and then choose **Apply** and **OK** to save and exit.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/01/img_5880356ed7e6a.png)
