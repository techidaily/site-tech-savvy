## **Option 1: Restart Windows Update Service**

**Note** : You must be logged on as**an administrator** to perform the steps below.

 1) On your keyboard, hit**Windows key** and**R** at the same time. Then type in**services.msc** and hit**Enter** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f9130cba51.png)

 2) Press**W** key on your keyboard to faster locate**Windows Update** service from the list.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f923a11cb9.jpg)

 3) Right click**Windows Update** and choose**Properties** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f9283d0507.jpg)

 4) Make sure the**Startup type** is selected as**Automatic** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f92cb9d4e0.png)

 5) Please also make sure the**Service status** is set as**Running** . If not, click the**Start** button to start it. Then hit**Apply** and**OK** buttons to save the changes.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f92f70cf89.png)

6) Please also make sure the following services are running:

**BitLocker Drive Encryption Service**

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f95958e8c3.png)

**DCOM Server Process Launcher**

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f95aba22d4.png)

**Network Connections**

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f96ad4fdf6.png)

**Windows Firewall**

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f96f247756.png)

 If their status is not Running, you can right click them and choose**Start** .

## **Option 2: Disable IPv6 in Network Center**

 1) On your keyboard, press**Windows key** and**X** at the same time, then choose**Network Connections** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f99d858612.png)

 2) Choose**Network and Sharing Center** on the right side of the pane.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb0061fd96.png)

 3) Find your connection, then click it to view its detailed information.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb05393ff0.png)

 4) Click**Properties** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb06cd3856.png)

 5) Locate**Internet Protocol Version 6 (TCP /IPv6)** . Click to**un-tick** the box before this option. Then click**OK** to save the change.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb092b47aa.png)

6) Restart your PC to see if this problem continues.

## **Option 3: Disable Network List Service**

 1) On your keyboard, hit**Windows key** and**R** at the same time. Then type in**services.msc** and hit**Enter** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f9130cba51.png)

 2) Press**N** key on your keyboard to faster locate the **Network List Service** service from the list. Right click it and choose**Properties** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f97e676a96.jpg)

 3) In the**Startup type** category, choose from the drop down menu**Disable** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f9852c81ec.png)

 4) Then make sure the**Service status** IS**NOT Running** . If it is, then click the**Stop** button. Then hit**Apply** and**OK** to save the changes.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f988166720.png)
